How it works

Three steps. None of them are yours.

  1. You save files like you always doNothing changes about how you work. Drop files anywhere in your Drive.
  2. The system reads and sorts themEvery hour, it looks at new files, figures out what each one is about, and picks the right folder — creating one if it doesn't exist yet.
